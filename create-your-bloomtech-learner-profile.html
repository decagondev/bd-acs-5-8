In addition to completing your career artifacts, you must also complete your BloomTech Learner profile.

The BloomTech Learner profile is the one source of truth that our recruiting team use when presenting your information to hiring partners and potential employers. It contains essential information such as links to your GitHub, LinkedIn, Portfolio, and Resume and information about your location, interests, skills, and project work.  

The good news is that you probably already have all the information needed in your career artifacts, so it is a matter of copy/pasting the information to your BloomTech Learner profile.

Follow these instructions to create your BloomTech Learner profile:

  1. Go to careers.bloomtech.com 
  2. Click on My Profile on the right hand corner
  3. Update Profile Photo
    • Click on the BloomTech logo to see the Intro Section  pop-up
    • Click on upload image to add your photo
    • Your name should already be entered and you have the option to type in a preferred first name (this is optional)
    • From the drop-down, select the location that you are closest to.
    • Since your email is already part of the main profile, you do not need to enter it here again.
    • Click SAVE
  4. Complete the following sections in your profile (click on the pencil):
    • Contact Info
    • Education
    • Industry Experience
    • Languages
    • Professional info.  Add links to your LinkedIn, GitHub, Resume.  Add your portfolio link if you have one, if not leave it blank.
    • Technical skills
    • Job preferences
    • Projects (add 2-3 portfolio projects)
