The Requirement

Submitting your career artifacts is a requirement for this unit, and you will be prompted to do so in the Sprint Challenge Section, on the page labeled Pass Career Artifacts.

The career artifacts that you must submit include:

  • GitHub profile
  • Resume in the required resume template format
  • LinkedIn profile

You should have completed the 3 artifacts during the core units.  

Feedback and Support

After you submit your career artifacts, you will receive feedback and have the opportunity to improve upon them.

What to do Next

Follow the instructions on the Sprint Challenge>Pass Career Artifacts page where you will be guided on how to submit your career artifacts and how the feedback process works.
