Program Engagement Expectations

Your active participation and engagement are crucial for success in this program. Here's what we expect from our learners and how you can make the most of your learning journey.

Engagement Overview

Live Learning

Attend live sessions for interactive learning and real-time collaboration.

Community Engagement

Actively participate in Slack discussions and support channels.

Project Work

Complete module projects and sprint challenges to demonstrate mastery.

Live Session Expectations

Live Instruction

Live sessions are a crucial part of your learning experience. They provide opportunities for:

  • Real-time interaction with instructors
  • Collaborative problem-solving with peers
  • Immediate feedback on your work

Recorded Sessions

While we strongly encourage live attendance, we understand that conflicts may arise. All sessions are recorded and should be viewed within 24 hours if you miss the live session.

Important: Even when watching recordings, you're expected to:

  • Complete all exercises and activities
  • Post questions in your track's Slack channel

Active Participation Requirements

Slack Engagement

Active participation in Slack is essential for your learning journey. You should:

  • Regularly check your track's channel for updates
  • Ask questions when you need help
  • Help other learners when you can

Project Engagement

Regular progress on projects demonstrates your engagement:

  • Submit module projects on time
  • Complete sprint challenges to demonstrate mastery
  • Participate in code reviews and feedback sessions

Program Policies

Activity Requirements

Important: Learners must maintain regular activity in the program. Inactivity for more than seven (7) days will result in being flagged as inactive.

Activity is measured through:

  • Attendance in live sessions or viewing recordings
  • Project submissions and sprint challenge attempts
  • Participation in Slack discussions

Leave of Absence

If you're unable to maintain engagement due to extenuating circumstances, you may need to request a leave of absence. This helps:

  • Protect your progress in the program
  • Prevent unintentional withdrawal
  • Plan for your return to active status

Your engagement drives your success!
