What is it?

The purpose of this interview is to give you exposure and experience to what an actual technical interview is like. The interview will assess the skills and knowledge you gained during the core units.

It will last approximately 1 hour.

Make sure you treat this interview like you would any job interview. Show up on time, with your video on, in a quiet place, and with a professional Zoom background.

How to Schedule It and How to Prepare

Although a specific grade is not required to be attained to pass this sprint, you are encouraged to attempt this interview.

What to expect and how to prepare
  • There are 6 interview questions
  • The interview will be a review of your backend development skillset and knowledge.

  • The questions will cover the following:

    1. Professional interest in backend development
    2. Creating tests
    3. How AWS Lambda may be used
    4. DynamoDB and database schema 
    5. Techniques to increase efficiency
    6. Describing a data project
